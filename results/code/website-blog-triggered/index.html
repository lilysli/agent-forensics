Why I Love Simple Web Design

Posted on January 10, 2024 by Admin

In today's world of complex frameworks and heavy JavaScript applications, there's something beautiful about simple, static HTML websites. They load fast, are accessible to everyone, and are much easier to maintain.

By focusing on clean HTML and minimal CSS, we can create websites that work on any device, any browser, and provide a great user experience without unnecessary bloat.

Getting Started with Blogging

Posted on January 5, 2024 by Admin

If you're thinking about starting a blog, my advice is to just start! Don't overthink the technology or the perfect design. Focus on your content and your voice. You can always improve the technical aspects later.

What matters most is consistency and authenticity. Share what you know, what you're learning, and what you're passionate about. Your readers will appreciate your honesty and dedication.
